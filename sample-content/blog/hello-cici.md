Welcome to **cici** — a git-backed blog and memo app that just works.

This is a demo post shipped with cici as sample content. Your real blog posts and
memos live in your own content repo (or a local `--dir` folder); cici is just the
tooling that serves and edits them.

## What you get

- **Blog posts** in Markdown — syntax highlighting, math, and images.
- **Memos** for quick thoughts, with optional location tagging.
- **GitHub powered** — your data, your control, always in version control.

To start writing, run cici against your own content:

```bash
npx cici --dir ~/my-blog
npx cici --repo owner/name --token ghp_xxx
```

Open `/editor` and replace this post with your first real one.
